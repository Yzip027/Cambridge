# Cambridge Geography – Recent Past Papers

## Overview

Cambridge offers Geography qualifications at two main levels through Cambridge
Assessment International Education:

| Qualification | Code | Target Age |
|---|---|---|
| Cambridge IGCSE Geography | 0460 | 14–16 |
| Cambridge International AS & A Level Geography | 9696 | 16–19 |

## Exam Format

### IGCSE Geography (0460)
- **Paper 1** – Geographical Themes (1 hour 45 minutes, 75 marks)
- **Paper 2** – Geographical Skills (1 hour 30 minutes, 60 marks)
- **Paper 3** – Coursework (not examined; internally assessed)
- **Paper 4** – Alternative to Coursework (1 hour 30 minutes, 60 marks)

### AS & A Level Geography (9696)
- **Paper 1** – Core Physical Geography (AS, 1 hour 30 minutes)
- **Paper 2** – Core Human Geography (AS, 1 hour 30 minutes)
- **Paper 3** – Advanced Physical Geography (A Level, 1 hour 30 minutes)
- **Paper 4** – Advanced Human Geography (A Level, 1 hour 30 minutes)

## Key Topics in Recent Papers

### IGCSE Geography (0460)
- **Theme 1**: Population and Settlement
- **Theme 2**: The Natural Environment (rivers, coasts, weather, climate, natural hazards)
- **Theme 3**: Economic Development and the Use of Resources

### AS & A Level Geography (9696)
- **Core Physical**: Hydrology and fluvial geomorphology, atmosphere and weather, rocks and weathering
- **Core Human**: Population, migration, settlement dynamics
- **Advanced Physical**: Hazardous environments, hot arid and semi-arid environments, tropical environments
- **Advanced Human**: Global interdependence, economic transition, environmental management

## Tips for Using Past Papers

- Start with the most recent papers as they best reflect current syllabus expectations.
- Use mark schemes alongside question papers to understand expected answer depth.
- Practice map-reading and data-interpretation skills with Paper 2 (IGCSE).
- Review examiner reports to learn about common mistakes and how to avoid them.
