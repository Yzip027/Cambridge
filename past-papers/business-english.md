# Cambridge Business English – Recent Past Papers

## Overview

Cambridge Business English Certificates (BEC) are English language qualifications
designed for professionals and business learners. They are developed by Cambridge
University Press & Assessment and are offered at three levels:

| Level | CEFR | Former Name |
|---|---|---|
| B1 Business Preliminary | B1 | BEC Preliminary |
| B2 Business Vantage | B2 | BEC Vantage |
| C1 Business Higher | C1 | BEC Higher |

Each exam tests Reading, Writing, Listening, and Speaking in a business context.

## Exam Format

### B1 Business Preliminary
- **Reading & Writing** – 1 hour 30 minutes (7 parts)
- **Listening** – approximately 36 minutes (4 parts)
- **Speaking** – 12 minutes (3 parts)

### B2 Business Vantage
- **Reading** – 1 hour (5 parts)
- **Writing** – 45 minutes (2 parts)
- **Listening** – approximately 40 minutes (3 parts)
- **Speaking** – 14 minutes (3 parts)

### C1 Business Higher
- **Reading** – 1 hour (6 parts)
- **Writing** – 1 hour 10 minutes (2 parts)
- **Listening** – approximately 40 minutes (3 parts)
- **Speaking** – 16 minutes (3 parts)

## Key Topics Covered in Recent Papers

Recent Business English papers typically cover:

- **Reading**: Business correspondence, reports, advertisements, articles, and charts
- **Writing**: Business letters, emails, short reports, and proposals
- **Listening**: Telephone conversations, interviews, presentations, and meetings
- **Speaking**: Business discussions, presentations, and negotiations

## Tips for Using Past Papers

- Work through papers under timed conditions to build exam stamina.
- Review the mark schemes to understand how answers are scored.
- Use the official audio files for listening practice at real exam speed.
- Focus on areas specific to your target level (B1, B2, or C1).
